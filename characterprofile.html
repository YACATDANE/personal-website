    • Overall characteristics & backstory: Dawn is a very kindhearted and gentle person, quite beautiful inside and out. But despite her well-mannered personality, she isn't afraid to stand her ground when necessary. She has the urge to help those in need, a very compassionate being. She doesn't have any attraction towards anyone at all, and she's quite confused about the topic of love. Centuries ago, Dawn used to live in Dreamtale as a royal with her two older brothers, distant father, and incredibly toxic mother. She knew of Dream (because he was known by almost everyone, even the royal family) but she was never able to meet him personally. One day she snuck out of the palace out of boredom, stumbling across a skeleton boy reading under the tree. Dawn wasn't familiar with Dream's brother, so she was oblivious to all the mean rumors about him. She asked what book he was reading, and the rest is history. The more she spent time with Nightmare, the more she was also able to get to know Dream better. Not long after, she met a girl sitting in the rain. She had her own umbrella, so she placed it above the two of them. She asked about her name, and soon they became best friends. She introduced her to the twins and she got along with them, although not so much with Dream. The four of them were a close friend group throughout their childhood. But that all changed when Nightmare was sixteen. He ate the apple, causing him to become corrupted by a vengeful spirit. Nightmare destroyed everything and everyone in his path. Dream opened a portal for Dawn and MD to escape before turning into stone statue. Dawn had to learn to survive on her own, but soon as she figured out how to create her own portals with her own magic, she created one that lead to Dreamtale to check on Dream, in hopes the spell was broken. Throughout those 500 years, she'd travel across the multiverse to search for MD and Nightmare, and then back to Dreamtale to check on Dream. It was starting to get difficult to wait and search all by herself, but she made some friends along the way. (Ink and Blue, who later on became a part of the group when Dream's spell broke). Until finally, as Dawn was about to leave Dreamtale after her daily rounds, she felt a surge of magic pulse from where Dream was. They hugged and caught up and blah blah blah. Dawn introduced him to Ink and Blue and they formed a small team called the Star Sanses. (Ink and Blue joined the party!) Then more years passed, MD found Dawn while she was AU hopping and recruited her as well.
