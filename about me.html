ABOUT ME

WHO AM I?

HI! I'm the co-creator of Starverse, Dane.

These past few months of building this website has always been a dream of mine ever since we created our au, our idea was mostly to fill in the missing spots of quarantine since we were bored and why not use this opportunity to make characters and ship them with canon ones? It started off as a rough idea of shipping ocs and gradually evolved to a more complicated au filled with sorrow, tragedy, action, teamwork, and romance. Thank goodness I chose an idea I love, or else I'd be deprived and bored. I'm just a “normal” 16yr old that decided to make an au because quarantine was boring, I draw a lot and make creative stories.

WHAT ISNPIRED ME TO MAKE THIS?

As I've said, I was bored. But if you ask in a more deeper level, we made this au due to a game called "Undertale". I was inspired by how complex and deep the meaning is despite the game only being 3 hours long if you exclude the fight scenes, as well as the fandom for creating alternate universes. The one I used was Dreamtale and it's a very beautiful backstory! Also why not make my best friend proud by making a website over our ocs, right?
